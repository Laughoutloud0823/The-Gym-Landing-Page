Superman

The origin story of Superman relates that he was born Kal-El
on the alien planet Krypton, before being rocketed to Earth as
an infant by his scientist father Jor-El, moments before Krypton's
destruction. ... Superman's love interest is generally Lois Lane,
and his archenemy is supervillain Lex Lutho.

Wonder Woman

Wonder Woman has used the alias Diana Prince, created by
William Moulton Marston, as her secret identity. During Marston's run, Diana Prince was the name of an army nurse whom Wonder Woman met.

Batman

Bruce Wayne is the only character to be identified as Batman
and is featured in Batman, Detective Comics, Batman and Robin, and Batman: The Dark Knight. Dick Grayson returns to the mantle of Nightwing and appears in his own ongoing series.
